Musqueam Indian Band

Successful yet respectful and humble

We address our people, and the people of all nations. We are Musqueam Indian Band, a Coast Salish nation who have been here since time immemorial. For more than 9,000 years we have called this place home. Our ways have kept us successful, and will continue to strengthen us through all the sunrises and sunsets yet to come. With good thoughts and intentions we can help the world, by sharing what we know with all our relations. Together we will get to a good place in a good way.


Musqueam Capital Corporation

Driven and intelligent, but caring

We address the Musqueam people first, and all our economic development relationship partners. We are an evolving team of economic experts, guided by Musqueam teachings as we walk through our days. Musqueam have always known that self-determination grows with economic independence. We were assembled to walk that path, clearing new ways toward success. We fuel a greater good for Musqueam, and all our relations. The good thoughts and intentions behind Block F are just one example.
