Positioning Statement: a narrative summary of the previous parts

Block F

Unique, inclusive and progressive

We address all people who search for more than a new home; people who thirst for a place where they belong. We are a new village, based on Musqueam culture and teachings, and all are welcome here. This village is a manifestation of our ways, born of 9,000 years of knowledge about about living with one heart and one mind. This has always been our home, and it always will be. Now it can be yours too. We offer a unique community, and your own home within it, where belonging truly comes first.
